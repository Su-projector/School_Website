Academics at Krelis Legacy School

Excellence across Primary and Secondary education

Nurturing Young Minds for a Brighter Future

At Krelis Legacy School, our Crèche and Nursery program is designed to provide a safe, stimulating, and nurturing environment where children can explore, learn, and grow. We focus on fostering creativity, curiosity, and foundational skills through play-based learning and age-appropriate activities.

Age Range: 1 to 5 years

Key Features:

  • Infant Care (Crèche): Personalized attention and care for infants in a loving and secure environment.
  • Nursery 1 & 2: Engaging activities that promote cognitive, social, emotional, and physical development.
  • Play-Based Learning: Hands-on experiences using toys, games, and creative play to develop early literacy, numeracy, and motor skills.

In addition to academic readiness, we emphasize character development, teaching values such as kindness, respect, and cooperation. Our goal is to lay a strong foundation for lifelong learning while ensuring every child feels valued and supported.

Subjects Offered:

  • Early Literacy
  • Basic Phonics
  • Numeracy (Counting & Numbers)
  • Creative Arts & Crafts
  • Music & Movement
  • Storytelling
  • Physical Education
  • Social Skills Development

Year 1 to 3 (Grade 1 to 3)

At Krelis Legacy School, our Lower Primary program (Years 1 to 3) is designed to build a strong academic foundation while nurturing curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking. We provide a structured yet engaging curriculum that prepares students for more advanced learning in the years to come.

Age Range: 6 to 8 years

Through a balanced approach of core academics, creative exploration, and character development, students develop essential literacy and numeracy skills, problem-solving abilities, and a love for learning. Our experienced teachers create a supportive and inclusive classroom environment where every child is encouraged to reach their full potential.

In addition to academics, we focus on values-based education, helping students develop respect, responsibility, and teamwork. We also integrate technology into the classroom to enhance learning and equip students with 21st-century skills.

Year 4 to 6 (Grade 4 to 6): Ages 9 to 11

At Krelis Legacy School, our Upper Primary program (Years 4 to 6) prepares students for the transition to secondary education by strengthening their academic foundation and fostering independent learning. We focus on developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and effective communication skills.

Age Range: 9 to 11 years

Our curriculum is designed to deepen students' understanding of core subjects while introducing more specialized topics. We emphasize literacy, numeracy, and scientific inquiry, equipping students with the skills they need to excel academically and adapt to new challenges.

In addition to academics, we promote leadership, responsibility, and teamwork through group projects, presentations, and collaborative learning. Students are encouraged to set goals, manage their time effectively, and take ownership of their education.

We also integrate technology into lessons to enhance digital literacy and prepare students for an increasingly tech-driven world. Our supportive teachers guide students as they build confidence, resilience, and a lifelong love of learning.

Year 7 to 9 (JSS 1 to JSS 3)

At Krelis Legacy School, our Junior Secondary program (JSS 1 to 3) provides a comprehensive and well-rounded education that prepares students for Senior Secondary School and beyond. We focus on academic excellence, critical thinking, and character development in a supportive and engaging learning environment.

Age Range: 12 to 14 years

Students are exposed to a broad curriculum covering core and vocational subjects, ensuring they develop both intellectual and practical skills. Our teachers employ interactive teaching methods, including experiments, projects, and discussions, to make learning dynamic and engaging.

In this stage, students begin to explore their strengths and interests through exposure to sciences, arts, technology, and entrepreneurship. We encourage self-discipline, responsibility, and leadership, preparing them for the Junior Secondary School Certificate Examination (BECE/JSCE). Our emphasis on moral values, teamwork, and problem-solving equips students with the skills needed to thrive academically and socially.

Year 10 to 12 (SSS 1 to SSS 3)

At Krelis Legacy School, our Senior Secondary program (SSS 1 to 3) is designed to prepare students for higher education, career success, and responsible citizenship. We offer a rigorous and well-structured curriculum aligned with national standards, providing students with the academic foundation they need to excel in their chosen fields.

Age Range: 15 to 17 years

Students can choose from three major academic pathways:

  • Science Track: For students interested in medicine, engineering, technology, and research.
  • Commercial Track: For students pursuing business, finance, accounting, and management.
  • Arts/Humanities Track: For students interested in law, languages, social sciences, and creative fields.

Our experienced teachers provide personalized guidance, helping students develop analytical thinking, problem-solving skills, and independent learning. We emphasize exam preparation for the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE) and the National Examination Council (NECO) exams.

Beyond academics, we focus on leadership development, entrepreneurship, and career counseling to help students make informed decisions about their future. Through extracurricular activities, mentorship programs, and community service, we prepare well-rounded individuals ready to make a positive impact in society.
